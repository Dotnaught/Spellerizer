Spellerizer

An extension for Google Chrome that allows you to replace words with US spellings in The Register articles with UK spellings.
  • Pin the extension so it's always visible.
  • Read The Register as you normally would.
  • Click on the extension icon in the toolbar.
  • Spellerizer will replace all US spellings in the built-in database with UK spellings.
  • It may make things confusing because it doesn't consider word context.
  • For example, it will replace "check" with "cheque," even when UK English would also call for "check."
  • The extension badge will show the number of words replaced, if any.
  • Reloading the page will undo any changes.
